Arts & Culture

Ponung Dance

Performed by the Adi tribe, this is an all-women group dance done before the harvest season to thank the gods. It depicts stories of crop growth and cultural heritage, led by a male singer called the Miri.

Cane and Bamboo Craft

Indigenous tribes craft baskets, bags, hats, belts, and containers using cane and bamboo, with intricate weaving unique to their traditions.
